About Education Ministry Circulars

The Ministry of Education (formerly MHRD) issues specific circulars and guidelines regarding implementation of the Right to Information Act, 2005 in educational institutions. These circulars provide specialized guidance for addressing RTI queries in the education sector.

These circulars apply to all educational institutions under the Ministry's purview, including central universities, IITs, NITs, central schools, and other autonomous educational bodies.

Significance of Education-Specific Guidelines

The education sector has unique RTI implementation challenges that require specialized guidance:

  • Handling sensitive student data while complying with RTI requirements
  • Managing RTI queries about admissions, examinations, and evaluations
  • Addressing scholarship-related information requests
  • Balancing institutional autonomy with transparency obligations
  • Implementation of RTI in educational research and innovation contexts

Key Education Ministry Circulars

Circular No.: F.No.1-14/2018-TS.I

Date: September 10, 2018

View Full Circular

Key Points:

  • Educational institutions must disclose complete admission criteria before start of admission process
  • Weightage given to different components of selection must be clearly specified
  • Cut-off marks for different categories must be published after admissions
  • Information regarding seats filled vs. available must be regularly updated on websites
  • Answer keys of entrance examinations should be published to ensure transparency

Circular No.: F.No.2-8/2019-TS.I

Date: May 17, 2019

View Full Circular

Key Points:

  • Guidelines for providing access to evaluated answer sheets under RTI
  • Standardized procedure for inspection of answer sheets by students
  • Digital copies of answer sheets should be provided when requested
  • Evaluation scheme and model answers must be disclosed after examinations
  • Institutions should establish transparent re-evaluation procedures

Circular No.: F.No.4-12/2020-TS.I

Date: November 23, 2020

View Full Circular

Key Points:

  • Educational institutions must maintain complete records of faculty selection process
  • Detailed minutes of selection committee meetings should be maintained
  • Criteria for awarding marks in interviews must be documented
  • Individual scores of candidates should be maintained for RTI disclosures
  • Guidelines for disclosing comparative assessment while respecting privacy

Circular No.: F.No.3-7/2021-TS.I

Date: August 9, 2021

View Full Circular

Key Points:

  • Institutions must maintain transparent record of scholarship disbursements
  • Selection criteria for scholarships must be published in advance
  • Lists of scholarship recipients should be proactively disclosed
  • Scholarship fund utilization reports must be maintained for RTI purposes
  • Guidelines for handling RTI queries about individual scholarship applications
