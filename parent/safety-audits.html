School Safety Audits

Ensure your child's school meets essential safety standards through RTI-driven monitoring

Why School Safety Audits Matter

School safety is a critical concern for every parent. While schools are expected to maintain comprehensive safety standards, many fail to comply with mandatory regulations. Using the Right to Information Act, parents can verify compliance, identify gaps, and ensure corrective actions are taken to protect children.

Benefits of Safety Audit Monitoring:

  • Verify compliance with mandatory safety regulations
  • Identify potential hazards before incidents occur
  • Monitor implementation of safety committees
  • Track safety drills and emergency preparedness
  • Ensure proper safety protocols for transportation and facilities

Key Safety Audit Categories

Structural Safety

  • Building stability certification
  • Earthquake resistance verification
  • Regular structural maintenance
  • Safe classroom design standards

Fire Safety

  • Fire NOC from local authorities
  • Functional fire extinguishers
  • Emergency evacuation plans
  • Fire drill frequency and records

Child Protection

  • POCSO-compliant committees
  • Background verification of staff
  • CCTV coverage and monitoring
  • Visitor management systems

School Safety Requirements

Functional Safety Committee

School has a formally constituted safety committee with parent representatives that meets quarterly

Valid Fire Safety Certificate

School has current fire NOC with annual inspection and quarterly drill records

Building Safety Certificate

Structural safety certificate from a government-approved engineer, renewed as per regulations

CCTV System

Functional CCTV cameras covering all critical areas with minimum 30-day storage of footage

Transport Safety

School buses with GPS tracking, speed governors, verified drivers, and female attendants

Staff Verification

Police verification for all staff including contractual workers like security, cleaning, and canteen

Water & Sanitation

Safe drinking water with testing certificates and adequate, clean, gender-separated washrooms

First Aid & Medical

Qualified nurse/doctor, equipped medical room, and staff trained in first aid and CPR

Safety Audit Process

1

File Initial RTI

Request safety certificates, committee details, and inspection records

2

Analyze Documents

Verify validity, completeness, and compliance with regulatory requirements

3

On-Site Verification

Conduct a walkthrough to match documentation with physical implementation

4

Identify Gaps

Document discrepancies between requirements and actual implementation

5

Submit Findings

Present concerns to school management with specific recommendations

6

Follow-up Audit

Verify implementation of corrective measures after agreed timeline

Sample RTI Questions for Safety Audits

Certificate Verification RTI

"Please provide copies of all safety certificates held by [SCHOOL NAME], including Fire NOC, Building Stability Certificate, Electrical Safety Certificate, and Water Testing Reports, along with their validity dates and issuing authorities."

State-wise Safety Regulations

State Key Safety Regulations Required Committees Inspection Frequency Enforcement Authority
Delhi Delhi School Education Act with 2017 Safety Guidelines School Safety Committee, POCSO Committee, Disaster Management Committee Annual comprehensive, quarterly fire safety Directorate of Education, Delhi
Maharashtra Maharashtra School Safety Policy, 2016 School Safety Committee, Transport Committee, Child Protection Committee Biannual for all safety parameters School Education Department
Karnataka Karnataka Educational Institutions (Certain Safety Measures) Rules, 2016 Child Protection Committee, Fire Safety Committee Annual by district-level committee Department of Public Instruction
Tamil Nadu Tamil Nadu Schools Regulation Act with Safety Guidelines, 2018 School Management Committee, POCSO Cell Annual by CEO, quarterly self-audits Chief Education Officer of the district
Gujarat Gujarat Child Safety Policy, 2017 School Safety Committee, Transport Committee Twice per academic year District Education Officer

Note: All states must also comply with the CBSE Comprehensive School Safety Guidelines (where applicable) and the National Disaster Management Authority's School Safety Policy Guidelines.

Safety Audit Success Stories

When our RTI revealed that our school's fire extinguishers had not been serviced for three years and emergency exits were blocked, we presented these findings to the management. The school immediately serviced all extinguishers, cleared exit pathways, and established a quarterly fire drill protocol that now involves parent observers.

Delhi Public School, Noida

Our safety audit through RTI uncovered that 40% of school staff, including bus drivers, had not undergone proper background verification as required by state regulations. After escalating this to the district education officer, the school completed all verifications and implemented a standardized onboarding process for all new hires.
