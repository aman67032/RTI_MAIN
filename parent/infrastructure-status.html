School Infrastructure Status

Monitor and verify school facilities to ensure quality learning environments for your child

Why Monitor School Infrastructure?

Adequate infrastructure is essential for quality education. Often, schools collect significant fees for infrastructure development but fail to provide facilities that meet regulatory standards. Through RTI, parents can verify if the school's facilities match what they're paying for and whether they comply with education board requirements.

Benefits of Infrastructure Monitoring:

  • Verify if facilities match what you're paying for in fees
  • Ensure compliance with regulatory requirements
  • Track utilization of infrastructure development funds
  • Monitor maintenance and upgrades of existing facilities
  • Compare your school's infrastructure with established standards

Essential Infrastructure Components

Academic Facilities

  • Classrooms with proper size and ventilation
  • Science laboratories with required equipment
  • Computer labs with functioning systems
  • Libraries with adequate books and resources

Sports & Recreation

  • Playground with minimum area requirements
  • Sports equipment and facilities
  • Indoor games and activity areas
  • Multipurpose halls for assemblies

Basic Amenities

  • Clean drinking water facilities
  • Separate, adequate toilets for boys and girls
  • Handwashing stations with soap
  • Proper waste management systems

Infrastructure Development Fee Regulations

Supreme Court Guidelines

The Supreme Court in the Modern School vs. Union of India case (2004) established that:

  • Development fees can be charged only if used for purchase/upgrade of fixed assets
  • Development fees should not exceed 15% of the total annual tuition fee
  • Schools must maintain separate development fund accounts
  • Complete transparency is required in utilization of development funds
  • No diversion of these funds for operational expenses is permitted
Citation: (2004) 5 SCC 583

State-specific Regulations

  • Delhi: Schools must justify any infrastructure fees with detailed project plans
  • Maharashtra: Infrastructure development fees capped at 15% of tuition fees
  • Tamil Nadu: Pre-approval needed from Fee Determination Committee
  • Gujarat: Fees must align with facilities provided under Fee Regulation Act
  • Rajasthan: Annual declaration of infrastructure fees and utilization required

Note: Check your specific state's fee regulation framework for applicable rules.

Infrastructure Verification Checklist

Land Area Verification

Verify if school has required minimum land area as per board norms

Classroom Infrastructure

Confirm number, size, ventilation, lighting, and furniture in classrooms

Laboratory Facilities

Check for required labs, equipment, safety measures, and usage frequency

Library Resources

Verify library size, book collection, reading area, and accessibility

Computer Facilities

Confirm computer lab setup, student-computer ratio, and internet access

Sports Facilities

Check playground area, sports equipment, and physical education resources

Sanitation Facilities

Verify toilet ratio, cleanliness, water supply, and accessibility

Special Needs Infrastructure

Confirm ramps, accessible toilets, and other facilities for differently-abled students
