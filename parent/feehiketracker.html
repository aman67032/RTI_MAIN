Why Track School Fee Hikes?

School fee increases often outpace inflation and go unchallenged due to lack of transparency and data. Our Fee Hike Tracker provides parents with the tools to monitor trends, identify excessive increases, and take informed action based on legal frameworks in your state.

Benefits of Using the Fee Hike Tracker:

  • Analyze your school's fee history across multiple years
  • Compare increases against legal limits in your state
  • Access benchmarks from similar schools in your city
  • Generate evidence-based reports for fee grievance committees
  • Collaborate with other parents through anonymized data sharing

State-wise Fee Hike Limits

Delhi

10% maximum with DoE approval required for schools on public land; must justify with financial statements

Maharashtra

15% maximum every two years; requires approval from Divisional Fee Regulatory Committee

Tamil Nadu

Fee fixed by Fee Determination Committee based on school classification into three tiers

Gujarat

Tiered caps based on location: ₹15,000/₹25,000/₹30,000 with Fee Regulatory Committee approval

Rajasthan

8-10% annual maximum increase; must disclose financial statements and get committee approval

Karnataka

Case-by-case regulation; fee increases must be commensurate with improved facilities

Steps to Challenge Excessive Fee Hikes

1

Track & Analyze

Document fee history and compare increases with legal limits and industry benchmarks

2

File RTI Application

Request justification, approvals, and financial statements related to fee increases

3

Form Parent Coalition

Organize other parents to collectively address concerns with school management

4

Request Dialogue

Meet with school management with data-driven arguments and legal references

5

Seek Legal Redressal

Approach Fee Regulatory Committee or Education Department with evidence

Sample RTI Questions for Fee Hike Scrutiny

Financial Justification RTI

"Please provide the financial justification for the fee increase of [PERCENTAGE]% for the academic year [YEAR], including the cost breakdown and the approval from the relevant fee regulatory authority."

Regulatory Compliance RTI

"Please provide copies of all approvals received from [STATE FEE REGULATORY COMMITTEE] for fee increases implemented in the last five academic years, along with the applications submitted for these approvals."

Download Template

Financial Statement RTI

"Please provide copies of the audited financial statements and income-expenditure accounts for the school for the last three financial years to assess the necessity of the recent fee increase."

Development Fee RTI

"Please provide details of development fee collection and utilization for the past five years, including specific infrastructure projects completed and planned using these funds."
