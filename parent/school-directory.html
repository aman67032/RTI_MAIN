Why Use the School Directory?

Our comprehensive school directory provides parents with detailed, verified information about schools across India. Whether you're researching schools for admission, comparing facilities, or gathering information for an RTI application, this directory serves as your one-stop resource for educational institution data.

Key Benefits of Our School Directory:

  • Access official recognition status and affiliation details
  • Verify compliance with RTE and safety regulations
  • Find correct authorities for filing RTI applications
  • Compare infrastructure and academic performance metrics
  • Identify schools participating in government schemes

Sample RTI Questions for School Information

Recognition Status RTI

"Please provide copies of all recognition certificates, renewal applications and approvals for [SCHOOL NAME] from [YEAR] to present, including any conditions or deficiencies noted during inspections."

Download Template

Staff Information RTI

"Please provide information regarding the total number of teaching staff in [SCHOOL NAME], their qualifications, appointment dates, salary scales, and compliance with minimum qualification norms."

Download Template

RTE Implementation RTI

"Please provide details of EWS/DG quota implementation in [SCHOOL NAME] for the past three academic years, including total seats, applications received, admissions granted, and reasons for rejections."

Safety Compliance RTI
