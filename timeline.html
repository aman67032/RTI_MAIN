RTI Timeline

Discover the journey of Right to Information in India's education sector

June 15, 2005

RTI Act Enacted

The Right to Information Act was enacted by the Parliament of India, empowering citizens to request information from public authorities.

October 12, 2005

RTI Act Implementation

The RTI Act came into full force, providing citizens a mechanism to question government authorities, including educational institutions.

March 23, 2008

First Major Education RTI Case

Landmark case where students used RTI to uncover admission irregularities in a prestigious university, leading to policy reforms.

July 8, 2010

RTI in School Education

Guidelines issued for implementing RTI in school education, making information about school infrastructure, teacher qualifications, and funds accessible.

November 14, 2013

RTI Amendment for Education

Specific amendments made to strengthen RTI implementation in educational institutions, requiring disclosure of key information.

April 21, 2016

Digital RTI Platform Launch

Launch of an online platform for filing RTI applications for educational institutions, making the process more accessible.

September 30, 2019

RTI 2.0 for Education

Comprehensive framework introduced to enhance transparency in education through improved RTI mechanisms.

February 12, 2022

National RTI Education Initiative

Launch of the National RTI Education Initiative to promote RTI awareness and usage in educational contexts across India.

Present Day

Ongoing Evolution

RTI continues to evolve as a powerful tool for transparency in education, with thousands of applications filed annually.
