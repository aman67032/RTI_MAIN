RTI Guide for Teachers

Learn how to use Right to Information effectively in the education sector

What is RTI?

The Right to Information Act (RTI) is a law enacted by the Parliament of India that provides citizens the right to request access to records of the government. It allows for transparency and accountability in the working of every public authority.

How RTI Works

Under the RTI Act, any citizen may request information from a "public authority" which is required to reply within thirty days. The Act also requires every public authority to computerize their records for wide dissemination and to proactively publish certain categories of information for easy citizen access.

RTI for Teachers

As a teacher, RTI can be a powerful tool to gain information about:

  • Recruitment processes and criteria
  • Promotion policies and timelines
  • Budget allocations for training and development
  • School infrastructure funds
  • Examination systems and evaluation criteria

How to File an RTI Application

1

Identify the Public Authority

Determine the specific government department or public authority that holds the information you seek.

2

Draft Your Application

Write a clear, concise application specifying the exact information you are requesting. Be specific about dates, departments, and details.

3

Pay the Required Fee

Attach the application fee (₹10) in the form of cash, postal order, or demand draft as specified by the department.

4

Submit Your Application

Submit your application to the Public Information Officer (PIO) of the relevant department either in person, by post, or online where available.

5

Track Your Application

Keep the receipt or acknowledgment and track the status of your application. The department must respond within 30 days.

Sample RTI Applications for Teachers

Recruitment Process Inquiry

To,
The Public Information Officer,
[Name of Education Department],
[Address]

Subject: Request for information under RTI Act, 2005

Sir/Madam,

Under the Right to Information Act, 2005, I would like to request the following information regarding the recruitment process for [position] conducted on [date/period]:

  1. The complete selection criteria and weightage given to different parameters
  2. Total number of applications received and shortlisted
  3. Cut-off marks/scores at each stage of selection
  4. Current status of recruitment and expected timeline for completion

I am enclosing the application fee of ₹10 via [payment method].

Yours faithfully,
[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[Contact Number]
