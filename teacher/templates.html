Teacher RTI Templates

Ready-to-use RTI templates designed specifically for teachers seeking information and advocating for their rights

Important: These templates are designed to help you access information that can improve your teaching environment and career growth. As a government employee (if applicable), you may need to consider internal grievance mechanisms before filing RTIs. See our FAQ section for more guidance.

Career Advancement & Service Matters

Templates to help teachers get information about promotion criteria, transfers, seniority lists, and other service-related matters.

Promotion Policy Information

Request detailed information about promotion policies, criteria, procedures, and historical implementation to understand your career advancement path.

Covers:

  • Official promotion guidelines
  • Selection committee composition
  • Weightage criteria for different factors
  • Previous promotion case statistics
View Template

Success Story

A group of 15 teachers used this template to uncover inconsistencies in promotion policies, leading to rectification of career advancement scales for over 200 teachers in their district.

Transfer Guidelines & History

Request information about transfer policies, procedures, historical implementation patterns, and specific transfers to ensure fair and transparent processes.

Covers:

  • Transfer policy documents
  • Criteria and point system
  • Exception handling procedures
  • Transfer committee composition

Seniority List Verification

Request complete seniority lists, the criteria used for determining seniority, and procedures for addressing discrepancies in the lists.

Covers:

  • Complete seniority list
  • Criteria for ranking
  • Service break handling
  • Grievance redressal mechanism
