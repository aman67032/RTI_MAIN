Effective School Administration Through RTI

Our resources will help you navigate the complexities of educational administration while using RTI to ensure transparency and accountability in the system.

RTI Case Studies for Administrators

Resolving Infrastructure Fund Delay

Problem

A government high school had been sanctioned funds for laboratory equipment under a central scheme, but the funds had not been released for over 8 months despite multiple follow-ups with district officials.

RTI Action

The principal filed an RTI seeking information on:

  • The current status of the sanctioned funds
  • The specific official(s) responsible for disbursement
  • The standard timeline for fund release
  • Any pending documentation required from the school

Result

Within 15 days of filing the RTI, the school received communication that their file had been expedited. The funds were released within the following week. The RTI response revealed that the file had been pending at a particular desk with no action taken.

Addressing Teacher Vacancy Crisis

Problem

A school had been operating with only 60% of its sanctioned teaching positions filled for over two years, severely affecting educational quality. Verbal requests for additional teachers had yielded no results.

RTI Action

The principal filed an RTI requesting:

  • District-wide data on teacher vacancies and distribution
  • The criteria used for teacher allocation among schools
  • Timeline for the next recruitment or transfer cycle
  • Minutes of the last district-level committee meeting that discussed teacher allocation

Result

The RTI revealed inequitable distribution of teachers across the district. Armed with this data, the principal presented the case to higher authorities, resulting in the temporary assignment of three additional teachers within a month. This case was later used in staff rationalization across the district.
