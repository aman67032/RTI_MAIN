Understanding Promotion Pathways

Entry Level - PRT / TGT

Primary/Trained Graduate Teacher positions typically require a B.Ed qualification and subject knowledge. Entry is usually through direct recruitment or contractual appointment.

RTI Tip

Request detailed recruitment rules and current vacancies in various schools to identify opportunities.

Get RTI Template

Progression to PGT

Post Graduate Teacher positions require a Master's degree in the relevant subject and typically 5+ years of teaching experience at the TGT level.

RTI Tip

Request information on promotion criteria, departmental exams, and the number of positions filled through promotion vs. direct recruitment.

Get RTI Template

Vice Principal / Principal

Administrative positions require significant teaching experience (typically 8-12 years) and often additional administrative qualifications or training.

RTI Tip

Request information on selection criteria, interview processes, and past selection committee composition for transparency.

Get RTI Template

Education Department Roles

Positions such as BEO, DEO, and other administrative roles in education departments offer career advancement opportunities for experienced educators.

RTI Tip

Request information on requisite qualifications, selection processes, and deputation opportunities.

Understanding Your Professional Rights

Seniority & Promotion Rights

Teachers have the right to transparent promotion processes based on clearly defined criteria. Seniority lists should be published regularly, and promotion orders must follow established guidelines.

Common RTI Questions
  • What is the current seniority list of teachers in my cadre?
  • What are the complete promotion rules for advancement from TGT to PGT?
  • How many vacancies exist at the next level, and what is the timeline for filling them?
Get RTI Templates

Transfer & Posting Rights

Teachers have the right to fair and transparent transfer processes. Many states have online transfer policies based on points systems considering factors like service in difficult areas, family circumstances, and tenure.

Common RTI Questions
  • What is the complete transfer policy for teachers in my state/district?
  • On what basis were transfers conducted in the last academic year?
  • What is the process for applying for transfer on compassionate/medical grounds?
Get RTI Templates

Deputation & Special Assignment Rights

Teachers have the right to be considered for deputation opportunities and special assignments based on fair selection criteria. These can include roles in education departments, project work, or specialized teaching assignments.

Common RTI Questions
  • What are the criteria for selecting teachers for deputation to education department roles?
  • How are teachers selected for curriculum development committees and textbook writing?
  • What is the process for applying for deputation to specialized educational projects?
