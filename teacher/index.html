RTI in Education: Challenges & Solutions

Teachers face various challenges that affect their ability to teach and ensure high education standards. RTI (Right to Information) is a powerful tool that can help teachers push for greater transparency, fairness, and accountability in the education system.

Pain Points of Teachers in the Education Sector

Lack of Proper Training & Professional Development

  • Limited access to modern teaching methods and digital tools
  • Insufficient subject-specific workshops and training on NEP 2020
  • No transparency on budgets allocated for teacher training

Lack of Transparency in Teacher Recruitment & Promotions

  • Opaque recruitment processes for teaching posts
  • Delays in recruitment announcements and unclear selection criteria
  • Unfair promotion policies or favoritism in appointing permanent positions

Lack of Transparency in Examination Systems

  • Non-transparent exam patterns and evaluation systems
  • Lack of clarity on how exams are graded or assessed
  • Limited access to answer sheets and post-exam procedures
