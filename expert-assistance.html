Why Expert Assistance Matters

While RTI is designed to be accessible to all citizens, navigating the complexities of the process can sometimes be challenging—especially when dealing with educational institutions that may be resistant to sharing information.

Our expert assistance services connect you with experienced RTI practitioners, legal experts, and education specialists who can help you craft effective applications, navigate the appeals process, and maximize your chances of success.

  • Detailed feedback on question framing
  • Suggestions for additional relevant questions
  • Verification of correct public authority
