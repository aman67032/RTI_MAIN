Frequently Asked Questions

You can check the status of your RTI application by visiting the "Track Application" section on our portal and entering your application reference number. Alternatively, you can call our helpline at 1800-110-111 for assistance.

For any technical issues related to the RTI portal, please email us at support@rtiindia.gov.in with screenshots and details of the problem you're experiencing. Our technical team will respond within 24 hours.

If you're not satisfied with the response to your RTI application, you can file a first appeal to the First Appellate Authority of the concerned department. If still unsatisfied, you can file a second appeal to the Central Information Commission through our portal or by visiting the CIC office.

We aim to respond to all inquiries sent through the contact form within 48 hours on working days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our helpline for immediate assistance.
