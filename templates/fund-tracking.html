Fund Tracking RTI Template

Use this template to request information about allocation, utilization, and monitoring of educational funds

Educational Funds Tracking RTI Application

This template helps you request detailed information about government funds allocated to educational institutions and their utilization.

To:

The Public Information Officer,
[Name of Department/Educational Institution],
[Complete Address]

Subject: Request for Information under Right to Information Act, 2005 regarding Fund Allocation and Utilization

Application:

Sir/Madam,

I, [Your Name], a citizen of India, would like to request the following information under the Right to Information Act, 2005 regarding the allocation and utilization of funds for [specify scheme/project/institution]:

  1. Total amount of funds allocated to [specify scheme/project/institution] for the financial year(s) [specify years].
  2. Detailed breakup of the fund allocation under different heads/categories.
  3. Copies of all government orders/circulars/notifications regarding the allocation and utilization of these funds.
  4. Statement of expenditure showing the utilization of funds allocated during the specified period.
  5. List of beneficiaries/institutions that received funds under this scheme/project, along with the amount received by each.
  6. Details of any unutilized funds, if any, and reasons for non-utilization.
  7. Copies of audit reports related to the utilization of these funds for the specified period.
  8. Details of monitoring mechanism in place to ensure proper utilization of funds.
  9. Information regarding any irregularities identified in fund utilization and action taken.
  10. Details of the committee/authority responsible for approving fund allocation and monitoring utilization.

I am willing to pay the necessary fee as prescribed under the RTI Act for obtaining this information.

I would appreciate if the information could be provided within the stipulated time as per the RTI Act, 2005.

Thanking you,

Yours faithfully,
[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[Contact Number]
[Email Address]
[Date]

How to Use This Template:

  1. Replace all text in [brackets] with your specific information.
  2. Add or remove points in the request list based on your specific requirements.
  3. Submit the application to the Public Information Officer (PIO) of the relevant department or institution.
  4. Keep a copy of the application for your records and request an acknowledgment.
  5. Follow up after 30 days if you don't receive a response.

Tips for Fund Tracking RTIs

  • Be specific about schemes: Mention the exact name of the scheme or fund you're inquiring about (e.g., Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan, Mid-Day Meal Scheme, RUSA, etc.).
  • Specify time periods: Always specify the exact financial years for which you need information.
  • Follow the money trail: If funds flow from central to state to district to school level, you might need to file separate RTIs at different levels.
  • Request physical inspection: For large documents like detailed expenditure reports, you can request physical inspection of records under Section 2(j) of the RTI Act.
  • Check utilization certificates: These documents show whether the funds were used as intended and are crucial for tracking misuse.
