RTI Application for Answer Sheet Access

Use this template to request access to your evaluated answer sheet(s). Click on highlighted fields to customize them for your specific situation.

Date: DD/MM/YYYY
To,
The Public Information Officer,
Name of Education Board/University
Complete Address
Subject: Application under Right to Information Act, 2005 for obtaining copy of evaluated answer sheet(s)

Respected Sir/Madam,

I, Your Full Name, a student of School/College Name with Roll No. Your Roll No., would like to request the following information under the Right to Information Act, 2005:

  1. A certified copy of my evaluated answer sheet(s) for the following examination:
    Examination: Name of Examination
    Subject(s): Subject Name(s)
    Examination Date(s): DD/MM/YYYY
    Result Declared on: DD/MM/YYYY
  2. A copy of the evaluation scheme/marking scheme/model answers used for the evaluation of the above answer sheet(s).
  3. Names and designations of the examiner(s) who evaluated my answer sheet(s).
  4. Details of the process through which I can apply for re-evaluation, if I find any discrepancy.

I am enclosing a Demand Draft/Indian Postal Order/Payment Receipt No. Payment Details of Rs. 10 as the application fee.

I hereby state that the information sought does not fall within the restrictions contained under Section 8 and 9 of the RTI Act and to the best of my knowledge, it pertains to your office.

As per the Supreme Court judgment in CBSE vs. Aditya Bandopadhyay & Others (Civil Appeal No. 6454 of 2011), evaluated answer sheets are subject to disclosure under the RTI Act, 2005.

Yours sincerely,

Your Name

Address: Your Complete Address

Phone: Your Contact Number

Email: Your Email

Tips for Success

Be Specific

Clearly mention all details of your examination including roll number, exam date, and subject. This helps in quick retrieval of your answer sheets.

File Promptly

Submit your RTI application as soon as results are declared. Some institutions have time limits for answer sheet preservation.

Keep Receipts

Always keep a copy of your RTI application and proof of submission (postal receipt or acknowledgment) for follow-up.

Legal Backing

CBSE vs. Aditya Bandopadhyay Case (2011)

The Supreme Court ruled that evaluated answer sheets are "information" under the RTI Act, and students have the right to access their own answer sheets through RTI applications.

What Next?

Wait Period

Expect a response within 30 days (35 days if sent by post). If no response is received, you can file a first appeal.

Review Carefully

Once you receive your answer sheets, check them thoroughly against the marking scheme for any discrepancies.

Re-evaluation

If you find discrepancies, apply for re-evaluation following the institution's procedures and timelines.
