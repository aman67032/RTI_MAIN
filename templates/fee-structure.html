Fee Structure RTI Template

Use this template to request information about educational institution fee structures and financial policies

School Fee Structure RTI Application

This template helps you request detailed information about school fees, including their breakup, justification, and approval process.

To:

The Public Information Officer,
[Name of School/Educational Institution],
[Complete Address]

Subject: Request for Information under Right to Information Act, 2005 regarding Fee Structure

Application:

Sir/Madam,

I, [Your Name], a citizen of India, would like to request the following information under the Right to Information Act, 2005 regarding the fee structure of your institution:

  1. Detailed breakdown of the fee structure for all classes/courses for the academic year [specify year], including all components such as tuition fee, development fee, sports fee, technology fee, etc.
  2. Copy of any government orders/notifications/circulars that regulate the fee structure of your institution.
  3. Details of any fee committee or regulatory body responsible for approving the fee structure, including meeting minutes where fee decisions were taken.
  4. Income and expenditure statements of the institution for the last three financial years.
  5. Details of any fee increases in the last five years along with justification for each increase.
  6. Copy of the balance sheet showing the utilization of funds collected as development fees, if any.
  7. Information regarding any special fees or charges collected for specific activities/events and their utilization.
  8. Details of scholarships, fee waivers, or concessions available to students and the criteria for availing them.
  9. Copy of any parent-school agreement or undertaking regarding fee payment.

I am willing to pay the necessary fee as prescribed under the RTI Act for obtaining this information.

I would appreciate if the information could be provided within the stipulated time as per the RTI Act, 2005.

Thanking you,

Yours faithfully,
[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[Contact Number]
[Email Address]
[Date]

How to Use This Template:

  1. Replace all text in [brackets] with your specific information.
  2. Add or remove points in the request list based on your specific requirements.
  3. Submit the application to the Public Information Officer (PIO) of the educational institution.
  4. Keep a copy of the application for your records and request an acknowledgment.
  5. Follow up after 30 days if you don't receive a response.

Success Story: Fee Transparency Achievement

In 2024, a parent group in Delhi used this RTI template to question arbitrary fee hikes in a prominent private school. The RTI revealed that the school had been collecting development fees but not maintaining a separate fund as mandated by law. The information obtained through RTI was used in a legal case that resulted in the school being directed to refund excess fees collected and establish proper accounting systems. This case benefited over 2,000 families and set a precedent for fee transparency in the region.
