Right to Information (Education)

Access information, ensure transparency, and empower education through RTI

This platform is your all-in-one space to explore, learn, and take action using the Right to Information (RTI) in the field of education.

Whether you're a student, parent, teacher, or just someone curious about how the education system works — this site helps you understand and use RTI to find answers, solve problems, and stay informed.

You'll find guides, real case stories, current issues, useful tools, and step-by-step help to file RTI applications with confidence.

We make RTI simple, accessible, and meaningful — for everyone.
