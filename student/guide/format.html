RTI Application Format

Standard formats for filing effective RTI applications for educational matters

Tips for Using These Formats

Replace all text in [square brackets] with your specific information

Customize the information requests to match your specific needs

Keep your application focused on one subject matter for best results

Be precise in your questions to avoid vague or incomplete responses

Standard RTI Application Format

Basic RTI Application

Date: [Current Date] To The Public Information Officer [Name of Educational Institution/Public Authority] [Complete Address] Subject: Application under Right to Information Act, 2005 Sir/Madam, Under the Right to Information Act, 2005, I would like to request the following information: 1. [Specific Question 1] 2. [Specific Question 2] 3. [Specific Question 3] 4. [Specific Question 4] 5. [Specific Question 5] I am enclosing the application fee of Rs. 10/- by [mode of payment: IPO/DD/online payment receipt]. Please provide the requested information within 30 days as per Section 7(1) of the RTI Act, 2005. Thanking you, Yours faithfully, [Your Name] [Your Complete Address] [Your Phone Number] [Your Email]

Important Notes:

  • Keep your questions specific and precise
  • Limit to 5-7 questions per application
  • Mention your contact details clearly

Educational RTI Formats

Examination RTI Format

Date: [Current Date] To The Public Information Officer [Name of Educational Institution/Examination Body] [Complete Address] Subject: Application under Right to Information Act, 2005 - Regarding Examination Results Sir/Madam, Under the Right to Information Act, 2005, I would like to request the following information: 1. Please provide a certified copy of my answer sheet for [Exam Name] with Roll Number [Roll No.] held on [Exam Date]. 2. Please provide the detailed marking scheme used for evaluating [Subject/Paper] in the above-mentioned examination. 3. Please provide information about the qualification and experience of the examiner(s) who evaluated my answer sheet. 4. Please provide the procedure for re-evaluation/re-checking of answer sheets and the timeline for the same. 5. Please provide the statistical data showing the number of students who appeared, passed, and failed in the above-mentioned examination. The above information is sought to understand the evaluation process and verify the correctness of my result. I am enclosing the application fee of Rs. 10/- by [mode of payment: IPO/DD/online payment receipt]. Please provide the requested information within 30 days as per Section 7(1) of the RTI Act, 2005. Thanking you, Yours faithfully, [Your Name] [Your Complete Address] [Your Phone Number] [Your Email]

Scholarship RTI Format

Date: [Current Date] To The Public Information Officer [Name of Scholarship Authority/Department] [Complete Address] Subject: Application under Right to Information Act, 2005 - Regarding Scholarship Information Sir/Madam, Under the Right to Information Act, 2005, I would like to request the following information regarding [Name of Scholarship Scheme]: 1. Please provide the current status of my scholarship application (Application ID: [Your Application ID]) submitted on [Date of Submission]. 2. Please provide the complete selection criteria and evaluation methodology used for awarding scholarships under this scheme. 3. Please provide information about the total number of applications received, shortlisted, and finally selected for the academic year [Year]. 4. Please provide the category-wise distribution of scholarships awarded. 5. Please provide the detailed timeline for disbursement of scholarship funds for the current academic year. The information is being sought to understand the scholarship selection process and the status of my application. I am enclosing the application fee of Rs. 10/- by [mode of payment: IPO/DD/online payment receipt]. Please provide the requested information within 30 days as per Section 7(1) of the RTI Act, 2005. Thanking you, Yours faithfully, [Your Name] [Your Complete Address] [Your Phone Number] [Your Email]

Admission RTI Format

Date: [Current Date] To The Public Information Officer [Name of Educational Institution] [Complete Address] Subject: Application under Right to Information Act, 2005 - Regarding Admission Process Sir/Madam, Under the Right to Information Act, 2005, I would like to request the following information regarding admissions to [Course Name] for the academic year [Year]: 1. Please provide the complete details of the selection process, including the weightage given to different components (entrance exam, interview, etc.). 2. Please provide the category-wise seat matrix showing the number of seats available in each category. 3. Please provide the category-wise cut-off marks/ranks for admission to [Course Name]. 4. Please provide information about my rank/position on the waitlist (Application No: [Your Application Number]) and the number of vacant seats still available in my category. 5. Please provide the detailed fee structure for the course, including all components and installment options if any. The information is being sought to understand the admission process and verify the correctness of my application status. I am enclosing the application fee of Rs. 10/- by [mode of payment: IPO/DD/online payment receipt]. Please provide the requested information within 30 days as per Section 7(1) of the RTI Act, 2005. Thanking you, Yours faithfully, [Your Name] [Your Complete Address] [Your Phone Number] [Your Email]

First Appeal Format

First Appeal Under RTI Act

Date: [Current Date] To The First Appellate Authority [Name of Educational Institution/Public Authority] [Complete Address] Subject: First Appeal under Section 19(1) of the Right to Information Act, 2005 Reference: RTI Application No. [RTI Application Number] dated [Date of RTI Application] Sir/Madam, I had filed an RTI application (details mentioned above) with the Public Information Officer (PIO) of your organization on [Date of RTI Application]. However, I am filing this appeal on the following grounds (select applicable): [ ] No response has been received within the stipulated time period of 30 days. [ ] Information provided is incomplete/incorrect/misleading. [ ] The PIO has rejected my request without giving proper reasons. [ ] I have been charged unreasonable additional fees. [ ] Other (please specify): [Your specific reason] Details of information sought and the issue with the response: [Provide specific details of the information requested and what was lacking in the response] I request you to: 1. Direct the PIO to provide the complete and correct information as requested in my original RTI application. 2. Waive any unreasonable additional fees charged (if applicable). 3. Take appropriate action against the PIO for not providing the information as per the RTI Act, 2005 (if applicable). I am attaching the following documents: 1. Copy of my original RTI application 2. Copy of the response received from the PIO (if any) 3. Any other relevant documents: [List any other documents] I affirm that the information provided by me is true to the best of my knowledge. Thanking you, Yours faithfully, [Your Name] [Your Complete Address] [Your Phone Number] [Your Email]

When to File First Appeal:

  • If you don't receive any response within 30 days
  • If the information provided is incomplete or incorrect
  • If your request is unjustifiably rejected
  • If you're charged unreasonable fees
