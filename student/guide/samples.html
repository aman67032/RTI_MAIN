Answer Sheet Copy Request

Examination
Date: 15th July, 2023 To The Public Information Officer Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) Shiksha Kendra, 2, Community Centre Preet Vihar, Delhi-110092 Subject: Application under Right to Information Act, 2005 - Regarding Class XII Answer Sheet Sir/Madam, Under the Right to Information Act, 2005, I would like to request the following information: 1. Please provide a certified copy of my answer sheet for the Class XII Physics examination (Code: 042) held on 5th March, 2023 with Roll Number ABCD1234567. 2. Please provide the detailed marking scheme used for evaluating the Physics paper for the 2023 CBSE Class XII examination. 3. Please provide information about the qualification criteria required for appointing examiners who evaluated the Physics papers. 4. Please provide the statistical data showing the number of students who appeared, passed, and failed in Physics for the 2023 Class XII examination. The above information is sought to understand the evaluation process and verify the correctness of my result as I have received unexpectedly low marks. I am enclosing the application fee of Rs. 10/- by Indian Postal Order (IPO) No. 38F 123456. Please provide the requested information within 30 days as per Section 7(1) of the RTI Act, 2005. Thanking you, Yours faithfully, Rahul Sharma 123, ABC Colony New Delhi - 110001 Mobile: 9876543210 Email: rahul.sharma@email.com

Notes on Success:

This application was successful in obtaining the evaluated answer sheet and marking scheme. The student discovered that 5 marks had been incorrectly totaled, leading to a revised score.

Application Status: Successful (Information Received within 25 days)

Scholarship Disbursement Inquiry

Scholarship
Date: 10th September, 2023 To The Public Information Officer Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment Shastri Bhawan, Dr. Rajendra Prasad Road New Delhi - 110001 Subject: Application under Right to Information Act, 2005 - Regarding Post-Matric Scholarship Sir/Madam, Under the Right to Information Act, 2005, I would like to request the following information regarding the Post-Matric Scholarship for SC Students: 1. Please provide the current status of my scholarship application (Application ID: PMSS-SC-2023-12345) submitted on 15th June, 2023 for the academic year 2023-24. 2. Please provide information about the total number of applications received from my state (Maharashtra) for this scholarship scheme for the academic year 2023-24, and how many have been approved so far. 3. Please provide the detailed timeline and process for the disbursement of scholarship funds after an application is approved. 4. Please provide information about any pending documentation or verification steps that may be causing a delay in processing my application. 5. Please provide the name and contact details of the grievance officer responsible for addressing concerns related to this scholarship scheme. The information is being sought as I have not received any update on my application status despite the academic year having started, and I am facing financial difficulties in paying my college fees. I am enclosing the application fee of Rs. 10/- by Indian Postal Order (IPO) No. 38F 987654. Please provide the requested information within 30 days as per Section 7(1) of the RTI Act, 2005. Thanking you, Yours faithfully, Priya Patel 456, XYZ Apartments Mumbai - 400001 Mobile: 8765432109 Email: priya.patel@email.com

Notes on Success:

This application prompted the authorities to expedite the scholarship processing. The student received information about her application status (which was "Pending Verification") and the exact documents that needed to be re-submitted. Within 3 weeks of filing this RTI, her scholarship was approved.

Application Status: Successful (Information Received and Issue Resolved)

Admission Waitlist Status Inquiry

Admission
Date: 5th August, 2023 To The Public Information Officer Delhi University North Campus Delhi - 110007 Subject: Application under Right to Information Act, 2005 - Regarding Admission to B.Com (Hons) Sir/Madam, Under the Right to Information Act, 2005, I would like to request the following information regarding admissions to B.Com (Hons) at Hindu College for the academic year 2023-24: 1. Please provide my current position on the waitlist for B.Com (Hons) at Hindu College (Application No: DU2023456789). 2. Please provide the complete category-wise seat matrix showing the total number of seats available for B.Com (Hons) at Hindu College. 3. Please provide information about the number of students who have confirmed their admission to B.Com (Hons) at Hindu College so far, and the number of vacant seats still available in the OBC-NCL category. 4. Please provide the detailed cutoff percentage/marks for each category for admission to B.Com (Hons) at Hindu College for the last three academic years (2020-21, 2021-22, and 2022-23). 5. Please provide information about the waitlist movement process, including when the next round of seat allotment is expected. The information is being sought to understand my chances of admission and to make an informed decision about accepting an offer from another college. I am enclosing the application fee of Rs. 10/- by online payment (Receipt No: DURTI78901). Please provide the requested information within 30 days as per Section 7(1) of the RTI Act, 2005. Thanking you, Yours faithfully, Amit Kumar 789, PQR Society Noida - 201301 Mobile: 7654321098 Email: amit.kumar@email.com

Notes on Success:

This application helped the student get precise information about his waitlist position (14th in OBC-NCL category) and that there were 8 vacant seats still available. The college also provided a timeline for the next round of seat allocation. Based on this information, the student waited and eventually secured admission in the next round.

Application Status: Successful (Information Received within 20 days)
