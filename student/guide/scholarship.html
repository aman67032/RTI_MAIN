Common Scholarship RTI Scenarios

Delayed Disbursement

If your scholarship payment is delayed beyond the specified timeline, you can file an RTI to inquire about the status and reasons for delay.

Sample Question:

"Please provide information regarding the current status of disbursement of [Scholarship Name] for the academic year [Year] and reasons for delay if any."

Rejection Clarification

If your scholarship application was rejected without proper explanation, you can seek detailed information about the rejection criteria.

Sample Question:

"Please provide specific reasons for the rejection of my application (Application ID: [Your ID]) for [Scholarship Name] and the evaluation criteria used for assessment."

Selection Criteria

To understand how scholarships are awarded, you can request information about the selection process and criteria.

Sample Question:

"Please provide the complete selection criteria, weightage given to different parameters, and the cut-off marks/percentile for [Scholarship Name] for the academic year [Year]."

Step-by-Step Process

1

Identify the Correct Authority

Determine which agency or department administers your scholarship.

For central government scholarships (like PMSS, CSSS), file with the Ministry of Education or relevant department. For state scholarships, file with the state education department or social welfare department.

2

Frame Specific Questions

Be precise about what scholarship information you're seeking.

Be Specific:

  • Include your application ID, scholarship name, and academic year
  • Ask for specific data points rather than general information
  • Limit each RTI application to one scholarship scheme
3

Submit Application

File your RTI application with the appropriate fee.
