Exam-Related Templates

Templates to help you access information about examination processes, answer sheets, evaluation criteria, and revaluation procedures.

Answer Sheet Access

Request access to view your answer sheets after exam results are declared. This template is designed based on successful cases where students obtained their answer sheets through RTI.

Covers:

  • Certified copies of answer scripts
  • Evaluation scheme/marking criteria
  • Model answers (if available)
  • Names of evaluators
View Template

Exam Re-evaluation

Request information about the re-evaluation process including timelines, fees, procedures, and previous revaluation statistics to understand how the system works.

Covers:

  • Re-evaluation guidelines and procedures
  • Success rate of previous re-evaluations
  • Qualifications of re-evaluators
  • Timeline for completion
View Template

Exam Data Analysis

Request statistical information about examination results, including pass percentages, grade distributions, and comparative performance data to understand evaluation patterns.

Covers:

  • Statistical breakdown of results
  • Subject-wise performance trends
  • Comparison with previous years
  • Moderation policies applied
View Template

Admission-Related Templates

Templates to help you get information about admission criteria, selection processes, cutoffs, and reservation policies.

Admission Criteria

Request detailed information about admission criteria, weightage systems, and selection processes for educational institutions. Useful for understanding how selections are made.

Covers:

  • Detailed admission criteria
  • Weightage for different components
  • Selection committee details
  • Previous year cutoffs
View Template

Merit List Information

Request information about merit lists, waitlists, and the criteria used for ranking candidates in competitive admissions. Helps verify fair selection processes.

Covers:

  • Complete merit list with scores
  • Tie-breaking criteria
  • Reservation category allocations
  • Waitlist movement data
View Template

Reservation Implementation

Request information about how reservation policies are implemented in admissions, including category-wise seat allocation and conversions if seats remain unfilled.

Covers:

  • Category-wise seat distribution
  • Unfilled seat conversion policy
  • Special category admissions
  • Government orders followed
View Template

Scholarship & Financial Aid Templates

Templates to help you access information about scholarships, their disbursement, selection criteria, and tracking delayed payments.

Scholarship Disbursement

Request information about scholarship fund allocation, disbursement timelines, and tracking mechanisms for approved scholarships that are delayed or not received.

Covers:

  • Scholarship disbursement status
  • Fund transfer tracking
  • Approval and payment stages
  • Responsible officials for delays
View Template

Scholarship Selection

Request information about how scholarship recipients are selected, including criteria, weightage systems, and selection committee details to ensure transparency.

Covers:

  • Selection criteria and scoring
  • Selection committee members
  • List of selected candidates
  • Budget allocation for scheme
View Template

Fee Structure Analysis

Request detailed breakdown of institutional fees, historical fee increases, and justifications for fee components to verify compliance with regulations.

Covers:

  • Detailed fee structure breakdown
  • Regulatory approvals for fees
  • Fee history over past years
  • Financial statements for verification
