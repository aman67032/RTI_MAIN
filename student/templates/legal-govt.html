Understanding Legal & Government RTI Applications

These templates help students access information from various government departments and legal authorities related to education. Use these templates to:

  • Request information about education policies and their implementation
  • Inquire about legal frameworks governing educational institutions
  • Get clarity on government funds allocation for education
  • Access information about regulatory compliance of institutions

Remember: When filing RTI applications to government departments, be specific and clear in your requests. This increases the chances of receiving the information you need.
