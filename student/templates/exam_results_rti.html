Exam Results RTI Template

Request information about exam evaluation, answer sheets, and marking schemes

When to Use This Template

This template is designed for students who want to inquire about various aspects of exam evaluation and results. Use this template when:

  • You want to request a copy of your evaluated answer sheet
  • You need information about the marking scheme applied in your exam
  • You want to know about the process of re-evaluation or rechecking
  • You need clarity on the moderation process used for exam results
  • You want to know about any grace marks policy
  • You need information about the number of students who passed/failed in a particular exam

Success Stories

RP

Rahul Patel

Delhi University Student

I was confident about my answers in the Economics final exam but was shocked to see I had barely passed. I filed an RTI using this template to get my answer sheet. When I received it, I found that two entire pages of my answers had not been evaluated! I submitted for re-evaluation and my marks increased from 58 to 79.

Result: Marks increased by 21 points after re-evaluation
AP

Aisha Parveen

CBSE Class 12 Student

Our entire batch had unexpectedly low scores in Mathematics. We suspected something was wrong with the evaluation process. I filed an RTI on behalf of our class to understand the marking scheme. The response revealed that an outdated marking scheme had been applied by mistake. The board initiated a re-evaluation for the entire batch.

Result: Re-evaluation ordered for the entire batch of 250+ students
SK

Sunil Kumar

Engineering Entrance Exam Candidate

I missed qualifying for my dream engineering college by just 2 marks. I filed an RTI to check if there was any grace marks policy or moderation that I might be eligible for. I discovered that the institute had a policy of giving 5 grace marks to students who had participated in national-level sports events, which I had! After providing the necessary documentation, I qualified for admission.

Result: Qualified for admission after application of eligible grace marks
