Scholarship Information RTI Template

Template for requesting information about scholarship selection, disbursement, and eligibility criteria

When to Use This Template

  • To get details about scholarship selection process and criteria
  • To understand delays in scholarship disbursement
  • To request information about your scholarship application status
  • To inquire about fund allocation and utilization
  • To request data on number of scholarships awarded across categories
Specifying the type helps in routing your application to the correct department.
If you select this option, you must attach proof of BPL status with your RTI application.

Declaration:

  1. I am a student who has applied for the above-mentioned scholarship
  2. I state that the information sought does not fall within the restrictions contained in Section 8 and 9 of the RTI Act, 2005
  3. I have paid the application fee as prescribed under the RTI Rules (or am exempt due to BPL status)

Success Stories Using This Template

National Overseas Scholarship Data Transparency (2023)

A group of students used RTI to obtain data on the National Overseas Scholarship selection process, discovering that funds were underutilized despite many eligible applicants. This led to policy reforms in the selection process.

Minority Scholarship Delay Resolution (2024)

After repeated delays in scholarship disbursement, students filed RTI applications that revealed administrative bottlenecks. This led to the implementation of a new online tracking system for all scholarship applications.

Research Grant Allocation Transparency (2022)

PhD students used RTI to understand the distribution pattern of research grants, which led to more equitable allocation across disciplines and institutions after discrepancies were highlighted.
