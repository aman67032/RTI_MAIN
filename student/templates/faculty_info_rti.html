Faculty Information RTI Template

Request transparency about faculty qualifications, vacancies, and teaching standards

When to Use This Template

This template is designed for students seeking detailed information about faculty members at educational institutions. Use this template when:

  • You need information about faculty qualifications and credentials
  • You want to verify faculty-student ratios or teaching workloads
  • You're concerned about faculty vacancies in your department
  • You need details about specialized faculty for certain courses
  • You want information about faculty selection process and criteria
  • You need to verify compliance with regulatory requirements for faculty
  • You want details about guest faculty or visiting professors

Sample Specific Information Request

I am requesting the following specific information regarding faculty at your institution:

  1. A comprehensive list of all faculty members in the [DEPARTMENT NAME] department for the academic year [YEAR], including their names, designations, qualifications, and specializations.
  2. Detailed information about the educational qualifications, research experience, and industry experience of the faculty members teaching the [COURSE NAME] program.
  3. The current faculty-to-student ratio in the [DEPARTMENT NAME] department, compared to the ratio prescribed by regulatory bodies like UGC/AICTE/MCI.
  4. Information about vacant faculty positions in the [DEPARTMENT NAME] department, including:
    • Number of sanctioned positions vs. filled positions
    • Duration for which these positions have been vacant
    • Alternative arrangements made to cover the teaching workload
  5. Details of research publications, patents, and funded projects undertaken by the faculty of [DEPARTMENT NAME] in the last three years.
  6. Information about guest faculty or visiting professors who taught courses in the [DEPARTMENT NAME] in the last academic year, including their qualifications and organizations they are primarily affiliated with.
  7. The workload distribution among faculty members in the [DEPARTMENT NAME], including the number of courses, teaching hours, and research supervision responsibilities assigned to each faculty member.

Key Points to Remember

  1. Be specific about the department: Specify the exact department or program for which you need faculty information.
  2. Request verifiable information: Ask for official documents like appointment letters, qualification certificates, or regulatory compliance reports.
  3. Compare with regulations: Reference specific regulatory requirements (like AICTE/UGC norms) for faculty qualifications or faculty-student ratios.
  4. Seek workload information: Request details about teaching hours, course allocation, and research supervision responsibilities.
  5. Ask about vacant positions: Inquire about unfilled faculty positions and steps being taken to address shortages.
  6. Request objective data: Focus on factual information like qualifications, experience, and publications rather than subjective assessments.

Success Stories

RK

Rahul Kumar

Computer Science Student

Our institute claimed to have 'world-class faculty' for a specialized AI course, but the quality of teaching seemed inconsistent. Using RTI, I requested information about the qualifications and experience of faculty teaching AI courses. The response revealed that only one out of five faculty members had formal qualifications in AI or Machine Learning. When this information was presented to the department, they were compelled to bring in qualified guest faculty from industry and arrange specialized training for existing faculty.

Result: Improved teaching quality with qualified AI specialists brought in
SP

Sunita Patel

Civil Engineering Student

Our department had been functioning with minimal faculty for over a year, affecting the quality of labs and project guidance. Through an RTI application, I found that 7 out of 12 sanctioned faculty positions were vacant, violating AICTE norms for faculty-student ratio. The RTI response also revealed that the institute had not advertised these positions for over two years despite collecting full fees from students. Following this revelation, the state technical education department intervened, and the institute was directed to expedite the recruitment process.

Result: Recruitment process fast-tracked with 5 new faculty members hired within a semester
AT

Ananya Thakur

Physics Graduate Student

My research required specialized guidance in quantum computing, but our department claimed they couldn't provide resources for this 'niche' area. Using RTI, I requested information about the research focus and grants received by the Physics department. The response revealed that the department had received a substantial grant specifically for quantum computing research two years ago. When confronted with this information, the department acknowledged the resources and established a quantum computing lab that became accessible to all interested students.

Result: Access to previously hidden quantum computing resources and specialized guidance
VR

Vivek Reddy

Journalism Student

Our media studies program advertised 'industry experts as faculty,' but we rarely saw these experts in our classes. Through an RTI application, I requested information about guest faculty appointments and teaching hours. The response showed that while the college had agreements with several industry professionals, they were only required to conduct 2-3 sessions per semester, not the regular involvement that was advertised. The students' association used this information to negotiate with the college, resulting in a revised program with structured weekly masterclasses by industry professionals in addition to regular faculty.

Result: Weekly masterclasses with industry professionals established as a formal part of the curriculum
