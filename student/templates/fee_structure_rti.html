Fee Structure RTI Template

Get transparency on educational fees, charges, and financial regulations

When to Use This Template

This template is designed for students seeking detailed information about fee structures and financial policies of educational institutions. Use this template when:

  • You need a breakdown of the total fees charged by an institution
  • You want to verify if fees comply with government regulations and guidelines
  • You suspect unauthorized fee hikes or additional charges
  • You need information about fee waivers, scholarships, or financial assistance programs
  • You require details about refund policies for various fees
  • You want to understand the justification for specific fees or charges
  • You need information about financial audits or regulatory compliance

Sample Specific Information Request

I am requesting the following specific information regarding fees at your institution:

  1. A detailed breakdown of all fee components for the [COURSE NAME] program for the academic year [YEAR], including tuition fees, development charges, laboratory fees, library fees, examination fees, and any other charges collected from students.
  2. Certified copies of the approval documents from the relevant regulatory authorities (such as UGC/AICTE/State Government) authorizing the current fee structure.
  3. Year-wise fee structure for the past 5 years (from [YEAR] to [YEAR]) for the same program, along with official justification for any fee increases during this period.
  4. Details of any additional charges collected from students that are not mentioned in the official fee structure.
  5. Complete information about fee refund policies, including eligible circumstances, refund percentages, and processing timelines.
  6. Details of the financial assistance programs, fee waivers, and scholarship opportunities available to students, along with eligibility criteria and application procedures.
  7. Allocation and utilization reports for development fees and other non-tuition fee components for the last two academic years.

Key Points to Remember

  1. Be comprehensive: Request information about all fee components, not just the total amount.
  2. Ask for approvals: Request copies of approvals from regulatory bodies that sanction the fee structure.
  3. Historical context: Ask for previous years' fee structures to identify unauthorized increases.
  4. Specify format: Request the information in a structured format that clearly shows each fee component.
  5. Regulatory compliance: Inquire about compliance with specific fee regulations applicable to your institution.
  6. Financial allocations: Ask for information about how specific fees (like development fees) are utilized.
