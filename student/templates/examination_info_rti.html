When to use this template

This RTI template is designed for students who need transparency regarding examination processes, answer sheet evaluation, re-evaluation procedures, and result calculation. Use this template when:

  • You want to understand the evaluation criteria for a specific examination
  • You need clarity on how marks are awarded or grades are calculated
  • You require information about examination policies and procedures
  • You want to verify the fairness of an examination or result

Key Points to Remember

  • Be specific - Clearly mention the examination, subject, date, and your roll number
  • Focus on facts - Request factual information rather than explanations
  • Include RTI fee - Attach the prescribed fee of ₹10 with your application
  • Timeline - Expect a response within 30 days of filing the RTI
  • Appeal rights - If unsatisfied with the response, you can file a first appeal within 30 days
