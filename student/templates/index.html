Why Use Our Templates?

Our carefully crafted RTI templates are designed to help you get quick and effective responses. Each template:

  • Targets specific information that educational institutions are legally required to provide
  • Uses precise language to minimize ambiguity and avoid rejection
  • Includes success metrics based on historical RTI data
  • Provides guidance on what supporting documents to attach
