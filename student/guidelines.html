RTI Portal Guidelines

Essential information for filing RTI applications online

General Information

  • This Web Portal is exclusively for Indian citizens to file RTI applications and first appeals online.
  • Applications can be submitted to Ministries/Departments of Government of India.
  • The portal facilitates online payment for RTI applications.

Application Guidelines

  • Click "Submit Request" to initiate a new RTI application.
  • Fields marked with * are mandatory.
  • Application text is limited to 3000 characters.
  • Allowed characters: A-Z, a-z, 0-9, and special characters (, . - _ ( ) / @ : & ? \ %)
  • For longer applications, use the "Supporting document" option to upload additional content.
Important: Do not upload Aadhar Card, PAN Card, or any personal identification documents (except BPL Card).

Payment Information

The following payment methods are accepted for RTI applications:

Internet Banking

Credit/Debit Card (Master/Visa)

UPI

RuPay Card

BPL Applicants: No RTI fee is required for citizens below poverty line. Attach appropriate government certificate as proof.

Process & Tracking

  • A unique registration number is issued upon submission for future reference.
  • Applications are electronically routed to the concerned Ministry/Department's Nodal Officer.
  • Track application status through "View Status" or email notifications.
  • If additional fees are required, CPIO will notify through the portal.
  • First appeals can be filed using the "Submit First Appeal" option (no fee required).

Important Notes

  • After payment, wait 24-48 working hours for registration number generation.
  • For payment issues, email helprtionline-dopt[at]nic[dot]in with transaction details.
  • PDF file names should not contain spaces.
  • Provide mobile number for SMS alerts.
  • All provisions of RTI Act, 2005 regarding time limits and exemptions apply.
